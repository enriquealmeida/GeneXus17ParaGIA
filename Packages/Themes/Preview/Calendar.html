Calendar control is composed by two controls, the calendar and a combo (which displays months and years). 'Calendar' class is associated to calendar control, and 'CalendarCombo' to the selection list.

For given example: 'today' is "July, 25th"; 'selected' day is "July, 29th".

  • Default calendar to pickup a date ('calendar' class)
  • Selection list ('CalendarCombo' class)
