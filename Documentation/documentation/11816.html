BPD Calendars

A calendar allows defining the time schedule and working days corresponding to workflow processes participants.  This allows adjusting Warning and Deadlines dates of processes and tasks exclusively to the defined calendar. Through the following dialog you can create the calendars associated to the knowledge base, once the calendars are created can be associated with activities and processes.
