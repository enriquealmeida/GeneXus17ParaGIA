RTL

RTL or Right to Left is a writing system where it starts from the right of the page and continues to the left. This can be contrasted against left-to-right writing systems, where writing starts from the left of the page and continues to the right. Arabic, Hebrew, Persian, and Urdu are some examples of RTL writing systems.
