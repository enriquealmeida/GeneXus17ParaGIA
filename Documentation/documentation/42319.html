RTL checklist

At least 200 million internet users speak a language that is written from right to left. Offering RTL styles in your developments open up your design so it can be used by these markets. Without them, that’s a good chunk of people currently missing out on your products and services (or your clients’).

Learn the importance of RTL styles and how to add them to your development. Follow these steps.

  • Review the Workstation Settings needed.
  • Review the Default KB language and Database Collation needed for internationalization.
  • Localize your product accordingly using the Language object.
  • Create an RTL Web Theme object and set the correct Base Style property.
  • Review all the steps from:
    • Getting ready for Right-to-Left Development
  • Thoroughly test your RTL generated application to make sure everything looks right.
  • Deploy and iterate.
