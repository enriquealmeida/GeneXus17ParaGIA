Adaptability Properties

This group of propeties helps you give greater flexibility to processes (in a Business Process Diagram's tasks), so that they adapt to different scenarios, including those that are less structured. 

They can be found under the node Advanced Properties> Adaptability:

Note that the last three properties are only available when the Is ad-hoc property=True.
