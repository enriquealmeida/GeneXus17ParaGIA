HowTo: Using ViewContact Method from Contacts for Smart Devices

This article will guide you through a simple use of the ViewContact method from Contacts.

To do our example we will consider the following Transaction object and apply the Work With for Smart Devices object (WWSD) pattern to it (see about this here: Applying Work With for Smart Devices Pattern).

Let’s assume that you have a list of clients. You have their contact information stored in your devices AddressBook. On the application you just have their name and last name.

Through this method you will be able to see the contacts info, stored in your device, in the application.
