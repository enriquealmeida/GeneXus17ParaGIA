Getting Started with watchOS and tvOS

Apple Watch and Apple TV are the new members of the Apple family that can run GeneXus applications. Most development concepts inherit from iOS. In case you are not familiar with developing applications for iOS with GeneXus, start here.

Even thow developing for watchOS and tvOS is similar to iOS, there are some important differences to take into account.

For each platform, see:

  • Getting Started with watchOS
  • Getting Started with tvOS
