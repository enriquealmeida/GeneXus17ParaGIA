Angular generator status

The Angular Generator has been released as of GeneXus 17 upgrade 4 and since then is being continuously updated on all GeneXus Delivery Channels: GeneXus Beta Channel, GeneXus Preview Channel, and GeneXus Release Channel.

Generator history

Since July, the generator is being released together with GeneXus, every two months.

July 2021 - First official release

With GeneXus 17 upgrade 4 the Angular generator has been released and got Generally Available.

March 2021 - Release Candidate

GeneXus 17 upgrade 3 included a Release Candidate of the Angular Generator

October 20, 2020 - Public Beta

Angular got Public Beta with the release of GeneXus 17. 

Oct / Dez 2019: Early Adopter Program

An Early Adopter Program was created so that those who had an iOS or Android app could get an Angular app from the same KB. Clients sent to the GeneXus development team their KB and they worked on the generator to generate a similar web application from the same KB and objects.
